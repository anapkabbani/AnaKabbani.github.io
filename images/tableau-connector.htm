Export store data to Tableau and build interactive sales reports and vizuals

Tableau Connector is an application for Tableau Shopify integration. It allows you exporting the key Shopify e-commerce data to Tableau in an easy way for further visualization and analytics. Tableau Connector is a no-code integration. The app allows creating URL connections easily, without any specific knowledge. Tableau Connector will be useful for Shopify store owners and managers who want to build custom Shopify reports (Shopify sales report, tax report etc).
